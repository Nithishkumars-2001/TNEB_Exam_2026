Results & Selection Process

Understand how results, answer keys, cut-offs, merit lists and verification stages work, in 60+ Q&A.

Typical result stages

Provisional answer key: Released shortly after the exam; objections can usually be raised within a fixed window.
Final answer key: Published after reviewing valid objections; used to calculate final scores.
Result & merit list: Category-wise cut-offs and ranks are announced on the official portal.
Certificate verification: Shortlisted candidates present original documents for eligibility confirmation.
Appointment: Final appointment orders are issued after successful verification and any required clearances.

Always remember

Only trust results and cut-off information published on the official TANGEDCO/TANTRANSCO portal. Unofficial sources can be outdated or inaccurate.
