Privacy Policy

A plain-language explanation of what information this site collects, how it is used, and how to keep your data safe during your exam journey.

Last updated: 2026

TNEB Exam Guide is an independent, educational website. We collect the minimum information necessary to respond to messages you choose to send us, and we never sell or misuse your data.

  • We do not require account registration to browse this site.
  • We never request Aadhaar, PAN or other sensitive government ID numbers.
  • Contact form data is used only to respond to your query.
  • This site is not affiliated with, and does not share data with, TANGEDCO or TANTRANSCO.
