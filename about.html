About TNEB, TANGEDCO & TANTRANSCO

Understand where TNEB came from, what TANGEDCO and TANTRANSCO do today, and why this context matters for your exam.

TNEB (Legacy)

The original Tamil Nadu Electricity Board, once responsible for generation, transmission and distribution as a single integrated utility.

TANGEDCO

Tamil Nadu Generation and Distribution Corporation — handles power generation and supply to consumers, including billing.

TANTRANSCO

Tamil Nadu Transmission Corporation — moves bulk power from generating stations to the distribution network via the grid.

A brief timeline

Formation era: TNEB is constituted as a unified state electricity utility for Tamil Nadu.
Growth phase: Expansion of generation capacity across thermal, hydro and later renewable sources.
Reform & unbundling: TNEB is restructured into separate corporations to sharpen accountability in generation, transmission and distribution.
TANGEDCO & TANTRANSCO today: Operate as distinct entities under the Tamil Nadu Energy Department, still popularly referred to together as "TNEB".
Present focus: Renewable integration, loss reduction, smart metering and reliable supply across the state.

Why this matters for your exam

General Knowledge Interview readiness Technical context Organisational awareness

Interviewers and General Knowledge sections occasionally test whether candidates understand the organisation they hope to join — its structure, functions and current priorities. A working understanding of TANGEDCO and TANTRANSCO helps you answer confidently and shows genuine interest in the role.
