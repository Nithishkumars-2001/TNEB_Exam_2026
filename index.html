ANSTNEB stands for Tamil Nadu Electricity Board, the original state body that generated, transmitted and distributed electricity across Tamil Nadu before it was reorganised.

ANSTANGEDCO (Tamil Nadu Generation and Distribution Corporation) handles power generation and distribution to consumers across the state.

ANSTANTRANSCO (Tamil Nadu Transmission Corporation) is responsible for transmitting bulk electricity from generating stations to distribution networks through the state grid.

ANSBecause TANGEDCO and TANTRANSCO originated from the erstwhile TNEB, and recruitment notifications are still popularly referred to as TNEB exams by aspirants and coaching centres.
